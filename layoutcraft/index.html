Tips for Better Results

1

Be Specific

Instead of "cool header," try "Modern tech company header with dark blue background and glowing geometric lines."

2

Include Text Content

Specify any text you want: "Header with the text 'Summer Sale - 50% Off' in bold white letters."

3

Define the Mood

Use descriptive words like "minimalist," "vibrant," "corporate," "playful," or "elegant."

4

Mention Colors

Guide the palette: "Use warm earth tones" or "bright green and gold accents."
