General Questions

Learn about LayoutCraft and how it works.

How is LayoutCraft different from other AI image tools? Popular

Unlike traditional AI image generators (like Midjourney or DALL-E) that focus on artistic interpretation, LayoutCraft is a functional design tool built specifically for business needs. Here's what makes us different:

  • Readable, Accurate Text: Our AI generates clean, properly placed text—not artistic gibberish. Perfect for headlines, captions, and CTAs.
  • Structured, Professional Layouts: We follow real-world design principles with proper hierarchy, spacing, and balance.
  • Business-Focused Output: Create usable assets like blog headers, social media posts, ad creatives, and email banners—not abstract art.
  • Multiple Format Generation: Pro users can generate multiple dimensions at once, optimized for different platforms.
Think of it this way

Other AI tools are like hiring an artist. LayoutCraft is like hiring a graphic designer who understands marketing.

Can I use the designs I create commercially? Popular

Yes, absolutely! You have full commercial rights to all designs you generate with LayoutCraft. This applies to:

  • Free plan users
  • Pro plan subscribers
  • Designs created during your 7-day free trial

You can use your designs for business marketing, social media campaigns, client projects, paid advertisements, print materials, websites, or any other commercial purpose. No attribution required, no additional licensing fees—ever.

How do I write a good prompt for LayoutCraft?

Great prompts are clear, specific, and descriptive. The more detail you provide, the better your results. Here's what to include:

  • Format/Purpose: "A blog header for..." or "An Instagram post announcing..."
  • Specific Text Content: "Include the title 'The Future of AI' and subtitle 'How it will transform marketing in 2025'"
  • Visual Style: "Modern and minimalist" or "Bold, vibrant, and energetic with geometric patterns"
  • Color Preferences: "Use a professional blue and white palette" or "Warm autumn colors with gold accents"
  • Mood/Tone: "Professional and trustworthy" or "Fun and playful for a young audience"

What design formats and sizes does LayoutCraft support?

LayoutCraft supports all major social media and marketing formats, including:

  • Social Media: Instagram posts (1080×1080), Stories (1080×1920), Facebook posts, Twitter/X banners (1500×500), LinkedIn posts
  • Web & Blog: Blog headers (1200×628), featured images, website banners
  • Marketing: Email headers, ad creatives, presentation slides
  • Print: Flyers, posters, and print-ready formats

Free users can export in standard quality PNG and JPG formats. Pro users get high-quality exports for perfect scalability and larger file size limits (up to 10MB vs 2MB on Free).

Feature Questions

Deep dive into LayoutCraft's powerful features and capabilities.

What is "Multi-Dimension Generation"? Popular

This is LayoutCraft's most powerful Pro feature—it saves you hours of repetitive work.

Instead of creating designs one at a time and manually resizing them for different platforms, you can generate multiple perfectly formatted versions from a single prompt.

Real-world example:

Write one prompt: "A product launch announcement for our new AI-powered calendar app, modern and professional style with blue accents"

Select dimensions: Blog Header (1200×628), Instagram Post (1080×1080), Twitter Banner (1500×500)

Click Generate → You instantly get three professionally designed, consistently styled assets, each optimized for its platform.

Time Saver

What typically takes 1-2 hours of resizing and reformatting now happens in seconds. Perfect for marketers managing multiple platforms.

What does "Unlimited Design Refinements" mean?

This is the heart of the Pro workflow—it gives you complete creative control.

After generating a design, you can use the same prompt bar to request changes as many times as you need. The AI understands your refinement requests and updates the design accordingly.

Example refinement requests:

  • "Make the title text larger and bolder"
  • "Change the background to a deep navy blue"
  • "Add a subtle geometric pattern behind the text"
  • "Move the logo to the top right corner"
  • "Use a more playful, rounded font"

Pro users can iterate infinitely until the design is pixel-perfect. Free users get 1 refinement attempt to try the feature.

Pro tip: Make one change at a time for best results. The AI handles incremental adjustments better than wholesale redesigns.

How does "Full History Access & Editing" work?

Both plans automatically save your design history, but with different levels of access:

Free Plan (1 month, view-only):

  • See thumbnails of all designs from the past month
  • Download any design you created
  • Cannot load designs back into the editor for changes

Pro Plan (6 months, full editing):

  • Access all designs from the past 6 months
  • Click "Edit" on any design to load it back into the designer
  • Make unlimited refinements to old designs
  • Generate new dimensions from existing designs
  • Re-export in different formats
Use Case

Created a blog header 3 months ago? As a Pro user, you can instantly load it, tweak the text for a new post, and generate matching social media versions—all without starting from scratch.

  • Direct Processing: Your credit card details are sent directly to Dodo Payments' secure servers—they never touch or pass through our servers
  • Bank-Level Encryption: All payment data is encrypted using industry-standard 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption during transmission
  • PCI-DSS Compliant: Dodo Payments meets the highest Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards
  • Zero Storage: We never see, store, or have access to your complete card information
  • Secure Infrastructure: Protected by enterprise-grade firewalls and security monitoring
Your Security Matters

Dodo Payments processes payments for thousands of businesses worldwide and maintains the same security standards used by major financial institutions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancellation is simple and hassle-free—no support tickets, no phone calls, no retention tactics.

Here's how:

  1. Log into your LayoutCraft account
  2. Click your profile icon and go to Account
  3. Click the "Manage Subscription" button

That's it! No questions asked, no forms to fill out. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep Pro access for the time you've paid for.

Changed your mind? You can easily reactivate your Pro subscription anytime from the same Account page.
