How is LayoutCraft different from other AI image tools?

Unlike traditional AI image generators that create artistic interpretations, LayoutCraft is specifically designed for functional design. We focus on:

  • Clean, structured layouts that follow design principles
  • Perfect text rendering - no more gibberish
  • Consistent, professional results every time
  • Formats optimized for real-world use (blogs, social media, presentations)

Think of it as the difference between an AI artist and an AI designer.

How do I write better prompts?

Great prompts include:

  • Format: "Blog header for..." or "Instagram post about..."
  • Specific text: "Include the text 'Summer Sale - 50% Off'"
  • Style/mood: "Modern, minimalist, professional, vibrant"
  • Colors: "Use blue and white" or "warm earth tones"
  • Elements: "Include geometric shapes" or "add subtle patterns"

Check out our complete guide to writing prompts for more tips!

What file formats are available?

Currently, all designs are exported as high-quality PNG files. PNGs are perfect for:

  • Web use (blogs, websites)
  • Social media posts
  • Presentations
  • Print materials

We're working on additional export formats for future updates.
