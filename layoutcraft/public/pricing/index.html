Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?

Sign up and get instant access to all Pro features for 7 days—no credit card required. If you choose to upgrade during or after your trial, your paid subscription begins after the trial period ends. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

What is Multi-Dimension Generation?

Instead of creating one design at a time, Pro users can generate multiple formats from a single prompt. For example, write "Product launch announcement" and instantly get a blog header (1200×628), Instagram post (1080×1080), and Twitter banner (1500×500) all at once. This saves hours of manual resizing and reformatting.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. Cancel from your account settings and you'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan with no loss of data.

What happens to my designs if I downgrade?

All your designs remain safe and accessible. You can view your entire design history (up to 6 months on Pro, 1 month on Free), but editing and refinement capabilities will be limited to Free plan features. You can re-upgrade at any time to regain full access.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day free trial so you can try Pro risk-free before committing. If you experience technical issues preventing you from using the service, contact our support team and we'll work with you to find a solution.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. Upgrade from Free to Pro at any time to unlock all features. If you downgrade from Pro to Free, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle, so you get full value from your payment.
